Overview

Data is the raw ingredient for surveillance, evidence, research and science. It plays a key role in informing interventions and policy decisions, and supporting core public health functions. PHACâ€™s Data Strategy represents an opportunity to examine our fundamental relationship with data and improve upon how we collect, analyze, share and manage our data. This interactive website will show you what we heard from employees across PHAC and how we plan to achieve the goals in the Data Strategy over the next five years.

Elements

The Data Strategy consists of six elements with specific goals and targets for each: 1) Data Governance; 2) Data as an Asset; 3) Data Infrastructure; 4) Science-based Analytics; 5) Partnerships and Collaboration; and 6) People and Culture.

Implementation Plan

Over the next five years, the PHAC Data Strategy has set specific actions, organized by phases, that the Agency will undertake in order to achieve our ultimate goal of PHAC being a world class organization with respect to public health data.
